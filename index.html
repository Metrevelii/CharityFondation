Alexi And Vakhtang Matchavariani Charity Found

Our Mission

Support of young Composers and performers of exceptional talent. Establishment of the International Composers Competition of Alexi Matchavariani.

  • Popularisation of Georgian Music in Georgia and Abroad.
  • Organisation of professional musical events and their support.
  • Establishment of the International Youth Symphony Orchestra.
  • Establishment of the musical Festival.
  • Restoration of damaged musical Scores of Georgian Composers.
  • Restoration of Audio & Video samples of old Georgian musical masterpieces.
  • Revival of forgotten Georgian musical masterpieces.
  • Presentation of unknown musical masterpieces in Georgia.
  • Connection of Georgian and foreign young musicians via Music.
